# Browsers

A collection of the best web browsers focused on privacy, speed, customization, security, and open-source software.

---

# Firefox

- Website: https://www.mozilla.org/firefox/
- Open Source: Yes
- Privacy Focused: Yes

## Why Use It?

Firefox is one of the last major independent browsers not based on Chromium.

### Unique Benefits

- Fully open source
- Strong extension ecosystem
- Highly customizable
- Better privacy controls than Chrome
- Backed by a non-profit organization

### Best For

Users who want a balanced browser with privacy, performance, and customization.

---

# LibreWolf

- Website: https://librewolf.net
- Open Source: Yes
- Privacy Focused: Extremely

## Why Use It?

LibreWolf is a hardened version of Firefox focused entirely on privacy and security.

### Unique Benefits

- Telemetry removed
- Strong anti-tracking defaults
- Enhanced fingerprinting protection
- Open source
- Firefox extension support

### Best For

Privacy enthusiasts who want strong protections out of the box.

---

# Brave

- Website: https://brave.com
- Open Source: Partially
- Privacy Focused: Yes

## Why Use It?

Brave is a Chromium-based browser with built-in privacy protections and ad blocking.

### Unique Benefits

- Built-in ad blocker
- Fast performance
- Anti-tracking by default
- Integrated Tor private windows
- Strong battery efficiency

### Best For

Users who want Chrome-like compatibility with better privacy.

# Ungoogled Chromium

- Website: https://ungoogled-software.github.io/ungoogled-chromium/
- Open Source: Yes
- Privacy Focused: Extremely

## Why Use It?

Ungoogled Chromium removes Google services and tracking from Chromium.

### Unique Benefits

- No Google integration
- Chromium compatibility
- Reduced background connections
- Open source
- Cleaner browser experience

### Best For

Users who like Chrome but dislike Google services.

# Tor Browser

- Website: https://www.torproject.org
- Open Source: Yes
- Privacy Focused: Maximum

## Why Use It?

Tor Browser routes traffic through the Tor network to maximize anonymity.

### Unique Benefits

- Anonymous browsing
- Hides IP address
- Strong anti-tracking protections
- Access to onion services
- Based on Firefox

### Best For

Users who need strong anonymity and censorship resistance.
