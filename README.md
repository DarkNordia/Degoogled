# DeGoogled

The best alternatives to Google products focused on privacy, freedom, and open-source software.

DeGoogled is a revival of taking back your time, privacy, and digital freedom online.  
Its goal is to collect, organize, and showcase the best alternatives to Google apps, services, and products — with a focus on open-source, privacy-respecting, and community-driven software.

Whether you want to fully leave the Google ecosystem or simply reduce your dependence on it, this repository helps you discover better options for everyday tools.

## Categories

-  Search Engines
-  Browsers
-  Email Providers
-  Cloud Storage
-  Maps & Navigation
-  Video Platforms
-  Android Alternatives
-  AI Tools
-  Password Managers
-  Productivity Apps
-  Messaging Apps
-  Music Platforms
-  Photo Storage

## Goals

- Promote privacy-focused software
- Support open-source projects
- Help users escape vendor lock-in
- Provide direct downloads and official links
- Make degoogling easier for everyone

## Contributions

Pull requests, suggestions, and new alternatives are welcome.
