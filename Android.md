# Android Alternatives

A collection of the best Android operating systems, app stores, and ecosystems focused on privacy, freedom, customization, performance, and open-source software.

---

## GrapheneOS

- Website: https://grapheneos.org
- Open Source: Yes
- Privacy Focused: Extremely

### Why Use It?

GrapheneOS is one of the most secure and privacy-focused Android operating systems available.

### Unique Benefits

- Hardened security features
- Minimal tracking
- Sandboxed Google Play support
- Frequent security updates
- Strong app permission controls

### Best For

Users who prioritize maximum security and privacy.

---

## CalyxOS

- Website: https://calyxos.org
- Open Source: Yes
- Privacy Focused: Extremely

### Why Use It?

CalyxOS balances privacy, usability, and compatibility with everyday Android apps.

### Unique Benefits

- Privacy-focused defaults
- Easy-to-use interface
- MicroG support
- Regular updates
- Reduced Google dependence

### Best For

Users wanting a balance between privacy and convenience.

---

## LineageOS

- Website: https://lineageos.org
- Open Source: Yes
- Privacy Focused: Moderate

### Why Use It?

LineageOS is the most popular custom Android ROM and supports a huge number of devices.

### Unique Benefits

- Massive device support
- Lightweight performance
- Customization options
- Long-term device support
- Open-source community

### Best For

Users reviving older Android devices.

---

## /e/OS

- Website: https://e.foundation
- Open Source: Yes
- Privacy Focused: Yes

### Why Use It?

/e/OS creates a Google-free Android experience with built-in alternatives to Google services.

### Unique Benefits

- DeGoogled Android ecosystem
- Built-in cloud services
- Privacy-focused defaults
- User-friendly interface
- App compatibility

### Best For

Users wanting a fully Google-free smartphone experience.

## DivestOS

- Website: https://divestos.org
- Open Source: Yes
- Privacy Focused: Extremely

### Why Use It?

DivestOS focuses heavily on security hardening and privacy improvements.

### Unique Benefits

- Security-focused patches
- Reduced telemetry
- Lightweight system
- Open-source development
- Privacy-focused modifications

Advanced users wanting hardened Android systems.

## F-Droid

- Website: https://f-droid.org
- Open Source: Yes
- Privacy Focused: Yes

### Why Use It?

F-Droid is an open-source Android app store focused on free and open-source software.

### Unique Benefits

- No tracking
- Open-source apps only
- No Google account required
- Community-driven
- Transparent software listings

### Best For

Users wanting open-source Android apps.

---

## Aurora Store

- Website: https://auroraoss.com
- Open Source: Yes
- Privacy Focused: Yes

### Why Use It?

Aurora Store lets users download apps from Google Play anonymously.

### Unique Benefits

- Anonymous app downloads
- No Google account required
- Clean interface
- Access to Play Store apps
- Lightweight client

### Best For

Users reducing dependence on Google Play Services.

---

## Obtainium

- Website: https://obtainium.imranr.dev
- Open Source: Yes
- Privacy Focused: Yes

### Why Use It?

Obtainium downloads Android apps directly from official developer sources.

### Unique Benefits

- Direct app updates
- No centralized app store needed
- Open-source focused
- Lightweight
- Flexible app management

### Best For

Advanced users wanting direct software control.

---

## MicroG

- Website: https://microg.org
- Open Source: Yes
- Privacy Focused: Yes

### Why Use It?

MicroG is an open-source replacement for Google Play Services.

### Unique Benefits

- Reduced Google tracking
- Better battery life
- Improved privacy
- Compatibility with many Android apps
- Lightweight background services

### Best For

Users wanting app compatibility without full Google services.

---

## Google Android

- Website: https://android.com
- Open Source: Partially
- Privacy Focused: Low

### Why Mention It?

Standard Android devices often include extensive Google integrations and telemetry.

### Downsides

- Heavy data collection
- Vendor lock-in
- Preinstalled Google services
- Tracking concerns

### Best Alternative Replacements

- GrapheneOS
- CalyxOS
- /e/OS
- LineageOS

## Final Thoughts

Modern smartphones collect enormous amounts of personal data.  
Choosing better Android alternatives can improve:

- Privacy
- Security
- Battery life
- Device longevity
- Freedom from Big Tech ecosystems

Even small changes like using F-Droid or Aurora Store can significantly reduce dependence on Google services.
