# AI Tools

A collection of the best AI tools, assistants, and platforms focused on privacy, open-source development, local execution, transparency, and independence from centralized ecosystems.

---

## Ollama

- Website: https://ollama.com
- Open Source: Partially
- Privacy Focused: Yes

### Why Use It?

Ollama makes running large language models locally extremely simple.

### Unique Benefits

- Run AI models locally
- No cloud dependency
- Simple setup
- Supports many open models
- Works offline

### Best For

Users wanting private local AI chatbots.

---

## Final Thoughts

AI tools are becoming a major part of modern computing.  
Choosing privacy-friendly and open alternatives can improve:

- Data privacy
- Local ownership
- Offline functionality
- Transparency
- Freedom from centralized AI platforms

Whenever possible, consider running AI locally to keep your conversations, files, and generated content under your own control.
