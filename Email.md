# Email Providers

A collection of the best email providers focused on privacy, encryption, security, reliability, and freedom from Big Tech ecosystems.

---

# Proton Mail

- Website: https://proton.me/mail
- Open Source: Partially
- Privacy Focused: Extremely

## Why Use It?

Proton Mail is one of the most well-known privacy-focused email providers, developed by scientists and engineers focused on secure communication.

### Unique Benefits

- End-to-end encryption
- Based in Switzerland
- Open-source apps
- No targeted advertising
- Strong privacy laws

### Best For

Users who want maximum privacy with a polished experience.

# Final Thoughts

Your email provider stores some of your most important personal information.  
Choosing the right service can improve:

- Privacy
- Security
- Productivity
- Ownership of your data
- Freedom from advertising ecosystems

If possible, avoid using email providers that rely heavily on targeted advertising or behavioral tracking.
